Frequently Asked Questions | 자주 묻는 질문

Is the procedure painful?

Most treatments involve minimal discomfort thanks to modern techniques and local or sleep anesthesia. Patient comfort is our priority.

How long is the recovery time?

Recovery varies depending on the procedure. Non-surgical treatments (like Thread Lift) are typically 3–7 days, while contouring surgery may require 4–8 weeks for major swelling to subside.

What is the price range?

Prices vary significantly based on the procedure, technique, and complexity. Please refer to our Services page for average price ranges. Your final quote will be provided after consultation.

What safety measures are in place?

We adhere to strict international safety protocols, utilizing state-of-the-art sterilization and monitoring systems, and have a certified anesthesiologist present for all surgical procedures.

Can I combine multiple procedures?

Yes, many patients choose to combine treatments for a comprehensive makeover. Our doctors will assess your health to ensure it is safe to do so.
