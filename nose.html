Nose Surgery: Harmonious Profile

Customized Rhinoplasty for the Golden Ratio Profile.

3D Profile Analysis & Customization

Rhinoplasty at iSeul goes beyond simple augmentation. We use advanced diagnostic tools to analyze the angle of the forehead, the projection of the chin, and the overall facial balance. This ensures your new nose complements your natural features, achieving the ideal "Golden Ratio" profile.

Dr. Park specializes in using **autologous cartilage** (from the ear or septum) combined with minimal prosthetic material for results that are safe, durable, and naturally soft to the touch.

Surgical Techniques | 수술 기법

  • Rhinoplasty (Bridge & Tip Augmentation)

    Focuses on enhancing the bridge using a combination of prosthetic material and autologous cartilage for the tip, ensuring a strong, defined look.

  • Alar Reduction (Nostril Narrowing)

    Reduces wide or flared nostrils to create a slimmer, more refined nose base that balances the width of the eyes.

  • Hump Reduction & Deviation Correction

    Smooths out bumps and corrects a deviated septum or crooked nose to achieve a straight profile and improve respiratory function.

Consultation Process

  • Step 1: Diagnostic Assessment. Detailed 3D-CT scan and facial analysis.
  • Step 2: Design and Planning. Consultation with the surgeon to finalize the shape, material, and profile goals.
  • Step 3: Post-Op Care. Comprehensive aftercare to minimize swelling and ensure rapid recovery.
