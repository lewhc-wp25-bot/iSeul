Anti-Aging & Lifting

Timeless Elegance: Restore Youthful Firmness.

Restore Your Youthful V-Line

Our lifting solutions are designed to address the signs of aging by restoring volume and firmness lost over time. We target sagging skin, deep wrinkles, and loss of jawline definition using the latest Korean lifting techniques.

We offer customized plans that may involve a combination of surgical methods for longevity and non-surgical procedures for subtle collagen stimulation and tightening.

Lifting Solutions | 시술 종류

  • Full Face Lift (Surgical)

    A comprehensive solution for significant sagging, addressing the **SMAS layer** for maximum, long-lasting results. Ideal for advanced signs of aging.

  • Mini Lift (Surgical)

    A less invasive surgical option with shorter incisions, primarily targeting specific areas like the mid-face or jowls. **Faster recovery time.**

  • Thread Lift (Non-Surgical)

    A non-surgical procedure using **dissolvable PDO threads** to lift tissues instantly, stimulate collagen, and improve skin texture.

Understanding Thread Lift Technology

Watch this video to understand how PDO threads are used to create structural support under the skin, giving you an immediate lift and long-term collagen production.

Thread lifts offer a minimal downtime solution, often called a "lunchtime lift," with dramatic results for patients with mild to moderate skin laxity.
