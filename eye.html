Eye Surgery: Bright Eyes, New Horizon

Customized Folds and Ptosis Correction for Expressive Beauty.

Natural Radiance & Personalized Fold Design

The key to beautiful eye surgery lies in precision that respects your unique facial structure and ethnicity. We specialize in creating customized double eyelids where the fold size and shape are meticulously tailored to achieve a charismatic and expressive gaze.

We assess eyelid thickness, fat volume, and the strength of the levator muscle before recommending the best approach.

Our Procedures | 주요 시술

  • Double Eyelid Surgery (Incisional, Non-Incisional, Partial)

    We offer methods tailored to your eyelid skin thickness and fat amount for a natural, long-lasting fold that opens the eye beautifully.

  • Ptosis Correction (Eye Muscle Adjustment)

    Corrects droopy eyelids by strengthening the levator muscle, improving both vision and aesthetics, making eyes look larger and more alert.

  • Canthoplasty (Corner Extension)

    Enhances the inner (medial) or outer (lateral) corners of the eyes to extend the width and create a softer, more open impression.

Recovery & Safety Focus

Post-operative care is crucial. We provide detailed guidelines on swelling management and incision care to ensure the quickest and smoothest recovery possible.

  • Typical Recovery Time: 5 to 10 days for major swelling.
  • Follow-up: Required on Day 3 and Day 7 to check healing progress.
  • Safety Priority: Dedicated surgical monitoring during the procedure.
