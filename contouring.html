Facial Contouring

Sculpted Perfection: Achieving the Ideal V-Line.

Safety & 3D Precision for Bone Contouring

Facial contouring surgery reshapes the underlying bone structure to create a smooth, feminine, and balanced face shape. It is the signature of Korean aesthetic procedures, requiring extreme precision and planning.

We utilize **3D CT imaging** to minimize risks to nerves and blood vessels, ensuring maximum safety and the most predictable outcome for V-Line, jaw, and cheekbone modification.

Contouring Procedures | 윤곽 수술 종류

  • Square Jaw Reduction (V-Line Side)

    Reshapes the mandibular angle to create a slim, smooth jawline from beneath the ear to the chin, achieving the desirable V-line effect.

  • Cheekbone Reduction (Zygoma)

    Reduces protruding cheekbones to soften the facial impression and narrow the overall face width, making the face look smaller and younger.

  • V-Line Surgery Combination

    A comprehensive approach combining jaw reduction and chin surgery for the ultimate slender lower face and defined jawline.
